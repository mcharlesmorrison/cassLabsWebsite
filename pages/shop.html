Cass Logger

The Cass Logger is a highly capable data logger for engineers and developers. It has eighteen analog channels accessed via six connectors on the device. The logger is highly configurable, allowing you to use our sensors, develop your own, and even write custom firmware. We have an open-source Github repository for data downloading and processing in Python. We will add support for other languages soon, but feel free to write your own!
